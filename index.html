About Me

My name is Miraz Hossain, and I am a feature writer at The Business Standard, a leading national newspaper. For the past two years, I have overseen a special weekly page called "Pursuit," which focuses on youth and workplace issues. In this role, I have curated over 75 issues, managing ideation, page layout, captions, and headlines. I also write in-depth analyses on political and national issues and produce long-form feature articles on human interest topics. My writing interests include AI, geopolitics, urban development, and environmental topics.
