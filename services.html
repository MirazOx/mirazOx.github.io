How it works

1 · Enquiry. Email a brief with scope, deadline, and word-count for translation or programme list for advising. I'll reply within 48 hours with availability.

2 · Scoping call. For anything over 5 hours of work, we set up a 20-minute call to agree deliverables, revision rounds, and a fixed price.

3 · Delivery. Drafts are delivered through email or a shared doc. Two revision rounds included; further rounds billed hourly.

4 · Payment. Invoice after delivery for projects under USD 200. Larger projects: 50% up front, 50% on delivery. Wise, bKash, or bank transfer.
